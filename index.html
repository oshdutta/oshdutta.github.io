About Me

I am a Ph.D. Scholar in Indian Institute of Technology, Delhi, India, working towards building resource efficient Deep Neural Network models and approaches to compress existing state of art models. My research is intended to reduce the cost of training and infering DNN models, such that complex computer vision and langauge models can be deployed to our power and space constrained handheld devices.

Education

My research work focusses on finding novel approaches towards building deep neural networks models that can be used for inference in devices with low memory and power ie., edge devices like microcontrollers.
